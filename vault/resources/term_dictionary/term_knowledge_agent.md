# Knowledge Agent

## Definition

A knowledge agent is an AI system that iteratively queries, retrieves, and reasons over large document collections to produce grounded answers. Unlike single-step RAG, knowledge agents use multi-turn interactions with search tools, maintaining conversation history and adapting their search strategy based on intermediate results. They require grounded reasoning -- reasoning that depends on access to knowledge beyond model parameters.
