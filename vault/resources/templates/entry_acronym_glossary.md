**Key Topics**: Logical Fallacies, Ad Hominem, Straw Man, False Dilemma, Slippery Slope, Appeal to Emotion, Appeal to Authority, Red Herring, Hasty Generalization, Circular Reasoning, Bandwagon, Tu Quoque, Post Hoc
