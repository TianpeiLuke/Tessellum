## Suggested Reading Order

A learning path from foundational thinking to applied research:

1. **Start here**: [The 5 Elements of Effective Thinking](../resources/digest/digest_effective_thinking_burger.md) — builds the meta-cognitive foundation; short (168 pp), practical, covers all five thinking habits
2. **Master the method**: [How to Solve It](../resources/digest/digest_how_to_solve_it_polya.md) — the foundational text on structured problem solving; Polya's four-step method (Understand → Plan → Execute → Look Back) and 67 named heuristics; applies to mathematics, engineering, research, and everyday decisions
3. **Deepen questioning**: [A More Beautiful Question](../resources/digest/digest_beautiful_question_berger.md) — expands the Air element into a full philosophy of inquiry with the Why → What If → How framework
4. **Sharpen question craft**: [The Art of Asking](../resources/digest/digest_art_of_asking_fadem.md) — practical techniques for asking better questions in professional settings
5. **Ground in science**: [Make It Stick](../resources/digest/digest_make_it_stick_brown.md) — cognitive science evidence for why elaborative interrogation, retrieval practice, and spacing work
6. **Apply to research**: [Where Research Begins](../resources/digest/digest_where_research_begins_mullaney.md) — channel thinking and questioning skills into a structured research process
7. **Understand your biases**: [Thinking, Fast and Slow](../resources/digest/digest_thinking_fast_and_slow_kahneman.md) — the cognitive science of why thinking goes wrong; System 1/System 2, heuristics, biases, and prospect theory
8. **Systematize execution**: [Strategic Problem Solving](../resources/digest/digest_strategic_problem_solving_hartley.md) — seven-step framework for taking problems from definition through decomposition, prioritization, and implementation
9. **Focus ruthlessly**: [The 80/20 Principle](../resources/digest/digest_80_20_principle_koch.md) — the meta-principle for all prioritization: 80% of results come from 20% of efforts; 80/20 Analysis and Thinking; the vital few vs. the trivial many; time revolution and recursive Pareto
10. **Adopt the growth mindset**: [Mindset](../resources/digest/digest_mindset_dweck.md) — the foundational belief system that makes sustained effort rational; fixed vs. growth mindset, praise effects, false growth mindset, and the "Not Yet" framework for reframing failure as a position on the learning curve
11. **Sustain the practice**: [Grit](../resources/digest/digest_grit_duckworth.md) — why passion and perseverance matter more than talent; the effort equations, four psychological assets (Interest, Practice, Purpose, Hope), and the Hard Thing Rule for building sustained commitment
12. **Integrate into life**: [The 7 Habits of Highly Effective People](../resources/digest/digest_7_habits_covey.md) — principle-centered framework for personal and interpersonal effectiveness; Maturity Continuum (Dependence → Independence → Interdependence); inside-out approach; Time Management Matrix for Quadrant II focus; Emotional Bank Account for trust-building
